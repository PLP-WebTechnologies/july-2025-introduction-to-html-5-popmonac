Nutrition Basics

Good nutrition fuels your body and mind. Focus on whole foods, balanced meals, and hydration.

Key Tips

  • Eat a variety of fruits and vegetables daily.
  • Include lean proteins and whole grains.
  • Limit processed foods and added sugars.

Exercise Essentials

Movement strengthens your body and boosts mental health. Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate activity per week.

Popular Activities

  1. Walking or jogging
  2. Strength training
  3. Yoga or stretching

Wellness Habits

Wellness goes beyond diet and exercise—it’s also about rest, stress management, and social connections.

Wellness Practices

  • Get 7–9 hours of sleep each night.
  • Practice mindfulness or meditation.
  • Maintain supportive relationships.

About This Guide

This guide was created to introduce the foundations of healthy living in a simple, practical way. Our goal is to make wellness accessible to everyone.
